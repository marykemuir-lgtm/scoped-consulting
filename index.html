About Me

The person behind the work.

I'm a delivery-focused BA and consulting professional with over 10 years of experience helping businesses untangle complex problems and get things moving.

I've worked across insurance, fintech, healthcare, retail, and supply chain logistics — in Auckland, across Australia, and in Dubai — which means I've seen enough broken processes, stalled projects, and misaligned teams to know exactly what good looks like.

People I've worked with tend to say the same two things: I'm detail-oriented, and I work fast. That combination is rare. It's also why the fixed-fee model suits me — I'm not incentivised to stretch an engagement. I'm incentivised to close it.
